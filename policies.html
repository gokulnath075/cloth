Policies

Shipping, returns, privacy, and terms of service

Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping: We deliver across India. Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days. Express delivery (1-2 business days) is available for select metros at ₹249.

Free Shipping: All orders above ₹1,999 qualify for free standard shipping.

Order Processing: Orders placed before 2 PM IST on business days are processed the same day. Weekend orders are processed on the next business day.

Return & Exchange Policy

30-Day Returns: We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags and packaging intact.

Free Exchange: Wrong size or color? We offer free exchanges on all orders. Simply initiate an exchange from your account.

Refund Process: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Amount is credited to the original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items: Customized or altered items, items marked as final sale, and underwear/innerwear cannot be returned.

Third Parties: We do not sell your personal data. We share necessary information only with shipping partners and payment processors to fulfill your orders.

Your Rights: You can access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@premium.in.
