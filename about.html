Our Story

Crafting excellence in men's fashion since 2020

The PREMIUM. Promise

Born from a desire to bridge the gap between luxury fashion and accessible pricing, PREMIUM. was founded by a team of passionate designers and textile experts. We believe every man deserves access to exceptional clothing that makes him look and feel his best.

Our journey began in a small studio in Mumbai, where we spent months perfecting our first shirt design. Today, we serve thousands of discerning gentlemen across India, each wearing a piece of our dedication to quality.

Our Craft

Every PREMIUM. shirt begins with the finest fabrics sourced from the world's most renowned mills — Egyptian cotton from the Nile Delta, Italian linens from Lombardy, and Japanese selvedge denims from Okayama. Our master tailors bring decades of experience to every stitch.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible fashion. Our supply chain prioritizes ethical sourcing, fair wages, and minimal environmental impact. We use eco-friendly packaging and continuously invest in sustainable manufacturing processes.
